SmartHive

This was an internet connected bee hive, whose key metrics could be monitored remotely.

I designed an assembly for the SmartHive prototype which would, for the purposes of testing, accurately simulate the conditions of a Langstroth hive.

The casing was laser cut out of acryllic and assembled using acryllic cement.
